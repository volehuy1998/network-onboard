## Dàn bài

## 12.1.1 Underlay BGP: iBGP vs eBGP route reflector patterns

*Sẽ phát triển: iBGP full-mesh vs eBGP per-switch ASN; route reflector vs confederation; unnumbered BGP RFC 5549; IPv6 link-local peering.*

## 12.1.2 Overlay VXLAN-EVPN integration

*Sẽ phát triển: underlay routes VTEP IPs; overlay MAC/IP learning; head-end replication vs ingress replication; BUM traffic handling.*

## 12.1.3 Vendor fabric comparison

*Sẽ phát triển: Cisco ACI (proprietary APIC controller); Arista CloudVision; Juniper Apstra (intent-based); comparison matrix.*

## 12.1.4 VNI scaling limit

*Sẽ phát triển: 24-bit VNI = 16M tenants theoretical; practical limits from hardware (TCAM size, MAC table); mitigation (VNI re-use with separate VTEP groups).*
