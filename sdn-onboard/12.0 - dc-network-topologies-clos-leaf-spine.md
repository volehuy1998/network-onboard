## Dàn bài

## 12.0.1 Clos original paper 1952

*Sẽ phát triển: Charles Clos Bell Labs 1952 "A Study of Non-Blocking Switching Networks"; three-stage architecture; theoretical non-blocking condition; circuit-switched origin.*

## 12.0.2 Leaf-spine modern incarnation

*Sẽ phát triển: two-tier leaf-spine; every leaf connects every spine; bandwidth scaling by adding spine; no north-south bottleneck.*

## 12.0.3 Fat-Tree (Al-Fares SIGCOMM 2008)

*Sẽ phát triển: Al-Fares/Loukissas/Vahdat "A Scalable Commodity Data Center Network Architecture" SIGCOMM 2008; k-ary Fat-Tree; cost analysis.*

## 12.0.4 ECMP hash functions

*Sẽ phát triển: 5-tuple hashing; flow-based ECMP; polarization problem; RSS on NIC side.*

## 12.0.5 Oversubscription ratios

*Sẽ phát triển: 1:1 non-blocking vs 3:1 vs 5:1 typical; cost/performance trade-off; hyperscale vs enterprise patterns.*
