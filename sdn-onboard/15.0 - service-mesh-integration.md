## Mục tiêu bài học

Sau khi hoàn thành phần này, bạn có thể:

1. **Mô tả (Understand)** service mesh architecture pattern: data plane (sidecar proxy hoặc node-level agent) + control plane (policy + telemetry).
2. **Phân biệt (Analyze)** ba approach chính: Istio (Envoy sidecar + xDS), Linkerd (linkerd2-proxy Rust), Cilium (eBPF native + no sidecar).
3. **Đánh giá (Evaluate)** trade-off sidecar vs sidecar-less, latency overhead, resource cost per pod.

---

## 15.0.1 Service mesh architectural pattern

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* L7 proxy pattern, mTLS termination, circuit breaker, retry policy, observability (distributed tracing, metrics).

## 15.0.2 Istio với Envoy sidecar + xDS

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* Istio control plane (istiod), Envoy xDS protocol (LDS/RDS/CDS/EDS), VirtualService + DestinationRule CRDs, Istio ambient mesh (sidecar-less mode).

## 15.0.3 Linkerd với linkerd2-proxy

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* Rust-based proxy (vs C++ Envoy), smaller footprint, simpler CRD model, Linkerd Buoyant Cloud.

## 15.0.4 Cilium eBPF-based service mesh

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* Cilium Service Mesh without sidecar (eBPF intercept at socket layer), integration với Envoy-only-when-needed, Hubble observability.

## 15.0.5 OVN-Kubernetes CNI

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* OVN làm CNI cho Kubernetes (thay thế kube-proxy), NetworkPolicy → OVN ACL translation, service mesh interoperability.
