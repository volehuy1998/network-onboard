## Dàn bài

## 15.0.1 ETSI NFV white paper October 2012

*Sẽ phát triển: white paper first edition tại SDN & OpenFlow World Congress Darmstadt Oct 2012; founding operator signatories (AT&T, BT, Deutsche Telekom, Orange, Telecom Italia, Telefónica, Verizon, China Mobile, NTT, KDDI, Vodafone); động lực chuyển từ proprietary hardware sang commodity x86.*

## 15.0.2 MANO stack: NFVO, VNFM, VIM

*Sẽ phát triển: NFVO (NFV Orchestrator) top-level; VNFM (VNF Manager) lifecycle management per VNF; VIM (Virtualized Infrastructure Manager) — OpenStack is typical VIM; interaction diagram.*

## 15.0.3 VNF → CNF evolution

*Sẽ phát triển: VNF based on VM; CNF (Cloud-Native Network Function) based on containers/Kubernetes; CNF design principles; operator adoption trend 2020+.*

## 15.0.4 Tie-in với OpenStack Tacker

*Sẽ phát triển: Tacker as OpenStack-native VNFM + NFVO; TOSCA template language; VNFD descriptor; integration với Heat for deployment.*
