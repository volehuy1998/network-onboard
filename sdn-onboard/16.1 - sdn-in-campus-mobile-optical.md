## Dàn bài

## 16.1.1 Campus SDN: Cisco SD-Access, Aruba

*Sẽ phát triển: Cisco SD-Access (LISP + VXLAN + CTS); DNA Center controller; Aruba CX Fabric; Cisco Catalyst Center (rebranded DNA Center 2023).*

## 16.1.2 Mobile packet core: 4G EPC → 5G UPF

*Sẽ phát triển: 4G EPC (MME, S-GW, P-GW monolithic); 5G Core Service-Based Architecture (SBA); UPF (User Plane Function) separation; vEPC/5GC deployment patterns.*

## 16.1.3 CUPS (Control/User Plane Separation) 3GPP

*Sẽ phát triển: 3GPP Release 14 CUPS (2017); S-GW-C/S-GW-U, P-GW-C/P-GW-U split; enables independent scaling; precursor to 5G SBA.*

## 16.1.4 Optical SDN: OpenROADM MSA

*Sẽ phát triển: Open ROADM MSA (Multi-Source Agreement) founded 2016; YANG models for DWDM disaggregation; AT&T/Verizon/Ciena/Nokia involvement; vs Open Optical Network.*

## 16.1.5 Service provider transport SDN: Transport API (TAPI)

*Sẽ phát triển: ONF Transport API (TAPI); T-API versions (v2.1, v2.4); multi-vendor optical management; northbound to OSS/BSS.*
