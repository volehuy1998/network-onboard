## Dàn bài

## 6.1.1 Motivation: TTP impractical cho multi-vendor deployment

TTP (Table Type Patterns, Part 4.5) đòi hỏi controller biết trước pattern của từng vendor switch, cứng nhắc. Flow Objectives ra đời trong ONOS khoảng 2015 để tránh: controller express intent dưới dạng high-level objective, ONOS driver layer translate xuống device-specific flow rule.

## 6.1.2 Three objective types: filtering, forwarding, next

FilteringObjective: "who can enter this pipeline", match VLAN + MAC + IP, allow/deny.

ForwardingObjective: "how to forward matched traffic", versioned (v1/v2/v3) để support different pipeline styles.

NextObjective: "next-hop group". ECMP, failover, indirect group abstraction.

Ba objective tạo thành layered forwarding model: packet entry (filter) → classify (forward) → next-hop (next).

## 6.1.3 Driver layer: objective → device flow mapping

ONOS driver cho từng vendor/pipeline translate Flow Objective sang OpenFlow flow entry phù hợp pipeline của device. OFDPA pipeline (Broadcom), dedicated driver; Corsa, another driver. Driver abstract hóa device-specific quirks khỏi controller app.

## 6.1.4 Current state: adoption hẹp ngoài ONOS

OpenDaylight không adopt Flow Objectives, dùng OpenFlow flow table trực tiếp. Vendor (Cisco, Juniper) prefer native API. Flow Objectives chủ yếu success trong Trellis SD-Fabric (ONOS-based underlay) + CORD (Central Office Re-architected as Datacenter) deployment ở telco. Bên ngoài, abstraction layer này ít phổ biến.
