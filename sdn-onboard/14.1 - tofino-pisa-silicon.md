## Mục tiêu bài học

Sau khi hoàn thành phần này, bạn có thể:

1. **Mô tả (Understand)** Tofino 1/2 silicon internals: stage pipeline, TCAM resources, ALU blocks, packet processing tốc độ 3.3 Tbps (Tofino 1) đến 12.8 Tbps (Tofino 2).
2. **Phân tích (Analyze)** PISA abstract model so với traditional fixed-function ASIC (Broadcom Trident), hiểu trade-off programmability vs silicon area (~10-20% overhead).
3. **Đánh giá (Evaluate)** lý do Intel EOL Tofino 01/2023 và implications cho P4 hardware ecosystem 2024-2026.

---

## 14.1.1 PISA reference architecture

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* Pipeline structure (ingress parser → MA pipeline → egress MA pipeline → deparser), stage resources (Match-Action Unit với TCAM + SRAM + ALU per stage), packet buffer architecture.

## 14.1.2 Tofino 1/2/3 generations

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* Tofino 1 (2016, 3.3 Tbps, 12 stages), Tofino 2 (2018, 12.8 Tbps, scale-up), Tofino 3 (2021, announced but low-volume), port configurations, power envelope.

## 14.1.3 Stage resources + compile constraints

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* P4 compiler p4c-tofino resource allocation, stage parallelism limits, table-to-stage mapping, register vs counter allocation, common compile errors.

## 14.1.4 Intel acquisition → EOL timeline

*Skeleton — nội dung sẽ bao gồm:* Intel acquire Barefoot 06/2019 (~$500M), Intel EOL announcement 01/2023, customer migration pathway (AMD Pensando, NVIDIA BlueField alternatives).
